About — 004 · The Founder

Hugh Mupfigo.
Engineer. Chef. Builder.

Former civil engineer turned builder of something more important: systems that prevent crisis instead of just managing it. SAFE is what happens when lived experience meets rigorous design thinking—when someone who survived the struggle spends a decade learning how to build spaces where young people don't have to.

Professional Statement

I've built things. I've fallen apart. I've healed. And I've learned that invisible structure—in buildings, kitchens, relationships, and systems—is what holds visible life together.

My work as a civil engineer taught me precision. My years in professional kitchens taught me leadership under pressure. My time in mental health support taught me humility. But my lived experience with crisis taught me why all of it matters. SAFE is the synthesis: a framework built by someone who has been the young person in crisis, who took the steps to heal, and who spent years understanding what actually works in real environments with real young people.

Every system I design, every course I teach, every template I create comes from this foundation: I know what it's like on the other side. I've walked the path. And now I design spaces and systems so others don't have to walk it alone, unprepared, or without hope.
