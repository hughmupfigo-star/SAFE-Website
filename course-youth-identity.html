Building Youth Voice & Identity

Helping Young People
Know Themselves.

Confidence and personal development through creative expression, honest self-reflection, and supported exploration. The Identity Studio approach teaches young people to ask: "Who am I when nobody is telling me?"

The Course

A

Identity is not given. It is discovered. This course teaches you how to create conditions where young people can explore who they are, express themselves safely, and build genuine confidence through authentic self-knowing.

01

6 modules. Creative approaches. Role-play and facilitation techniques. Case studies from real programmes.

5–6 weeks. 3–4 hours per week. Practical and interactive.
03
Who It's For
Youth workers, teachers, arts educators, anyone supporting young people's personal development.
04

Facilitation skills for identity work. Creative activity toolkit. Confidence in supporting self-exploration.

What You'll Learn

B

Theory & Psychology

  • How identity develops in adolescence
  • The role of creative expression in self-knowing
  • Cultural identity and intersectionality
  • Building genuine confidence vs. false self

Practice & Facilitation

  • Creating safe containers for self-exploration
  • Art, movement, and music as identity tools
  • Questioning and reflection techniques
  • Supporting vulnerable moments and breakthroughs

Curriculum

C

Week 1: Identity in Adolescence
Identity development stages. The critical role of authenticity and self-expression. Why young people struggle with "who am I?"
Week 2: The Identity Studio Framework
Core principles. Safe container creation. How dress, movement, and expression work together.
Week 3: Creative Tools
Art, fashion, photography, journaling. How to use creative mediums for identity exploration.
Week 4: Facilitation Skills
Questions that open doors. Witnessing without judgment. Holding space for difficult moments.
Week 5: Real World Application
Designing identity work in your setting. Group dynamics. Working with diverse young people.
Week 6: Workshop & Planning
Bring your own identity session ideas. Get feedback. Plan your implementation.
